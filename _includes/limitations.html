Service Limitations

To ensure safety, compliance, and proper disposal, certain items and materials are outside the scope of our standard clearance services.

Items We Do Not Handle

  • Hazardous chemicals and toxic substances
  • Asbestos or suspected asbestos materials
  • Explosive or highly flammable materials
  • Illegal or prohibited items

Operational Boundaries

  • Specialist industrial decontamination
  • Structural demolition work
  • Deep environmental remediation
  • Highly regulated biohazard removal
